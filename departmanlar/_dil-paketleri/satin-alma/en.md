| Supplier | supplier | Tedarikçi | Company providing goods/services |
| Vendor | vendor | Satıcı | Party selling products |
| Quotation | quotation | Teklif | Price quote document |
| Invoice | invoice | Fatura | Official payment document |
| Delivery | delivery | Teslimat | Handover of goods |

## Kısaltmalar
- **PO** — Purchase Order
- **RFQ** — Request for Quotation
- **GRN** — Goods Received Note
- **MOQ** — Minimum Order Quantity
- **EOQ** — Economic Order Quantity
- **COGS** — Cost of Goods Sold
- **AP** — Accounts Payable
- **SKU** — Stock Keeping Unit
- **PR** — Purchase Requisition
- **KPI** — Key Performance Indicator
