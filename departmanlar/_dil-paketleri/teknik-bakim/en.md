## Kısaltmalar
- AC = Air Conditioner
- VRF = Variable Refrigerant Flow
- HVAC = Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning
- R410A = Refrigerant gas type
- R32 = Next-gen refrigerant gas
- WC = Water Closet (toilet)
- PPR = Polypropylene Random (plastic water pipe)
- PEX = Cross-linked Polyethylene (flexible water pipe)
- PVC = Polyvinyl Chloride (plastic pipe)
- GFCI = Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter

## Yaygın Hatalı Yazılışlar / Noisy Input
- "AC broken" → correct: "AC malfunctioning" (specify fault type)
- "not cooling" → correct: "AC not cooling" (gas low/compressor issue)
- "gas refill" → correct: "refrigerant gas recharge" (R410A/R32)
- "water dripping" → correct: "AC indoor unit dripping" or "pipe leaking"
- "split" → correct: "split AC" (ambiguous standalone)
- "compressor noise" → correct: "compressor making abnormal noise"
- "faucet broken" → correct: "faucet needs repair"
- "heater not working" → correct: "water heater not working"
- "toilet clogged" → correct: "WC/toilet clogged"
- "paint peeling" → correct: "wall/exterior paint peeling"
- "lock broken" → correct: "door lock malfunctioning"
- "maintenance due" → correct: "periodic maintenance due"
- "leak" → correct: specify — "water leak" or "refrigerant leak" or "gas leak"
- "pipe burst" → correct: "water pipe burst (emergency)"
