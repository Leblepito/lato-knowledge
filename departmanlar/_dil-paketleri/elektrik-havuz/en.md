## Kısaltmalar
- RCD = Residual Current Device (earth leakage protection)
- ATS = Automatic Transfer Switch (mains-generator switching)
- SPD = Surge Protective Device (lightning/surge protection)
- VFD = Variable Frequency Drive (motor speed control)
- kWh = Kilowatt-hour (electricity consumption unit)
- kVA = Kilovolt-ampere (apparent power unit)
- PFC = Power Factor Correction (compensation)
- MCB = Miniature Circuit Breaker
- MCCB = Molded Case Circuit Breaker
- TDS = Total Dissolved Solids (water quality metric)
- ORP = Oxidation-Reduction Potential (water sanitization metric)
