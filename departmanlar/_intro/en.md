## How to Use Me

1. **Mention me**: Type `@Latotry_bot` then your question
2. **Write in the right topic**: I auto-route to the correct department
3. **Any language**: Turkish, English, Thai — I reply in your language
4. **Send photos/videos**: Fault photos, panel images, motor nameplates — I analyze them
5. **Send voice**: Voice messages in any supported language — I understand and respond

## Voice / Broken Messages

If you send incomplete or garbled voice/text messages:
- I make sense of them — fill gaps, correct misspellings
- I identify the department and route correctly
- Mixed languages (e.g. half Thai half English) — I still understand
